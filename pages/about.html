About Me


"Hey there! I'm Rahul Duhan, an agriculture student with a love for technology and open source software. I'm always trying to learn new things and improve my skills in web design, computer languages and other areas of technology. I'm a huge fan of Linux and Android and love to customize them to my liking.

I'm not just into agriculture for the field work, but also exploring new ways to improve the industry with the help of technology. I believe that by combining my love for technology and agriculture, I can find new ways to improve the industry and make a real impact. I'm excited to share my journey with you as I explore the intersection of technology and agriculture and see what kind of cool stuff I can come up with.

When I'm not in front of my computer, you can find me drawing cartoons or listening to J-pop all the time, it's my favorite music. Drawing cartoons is my way of expressing my creativity and it helps me to come up with new ideas and solutions.

So join me on this journey, let's see what we can achieve together and make a real impact in the world."
